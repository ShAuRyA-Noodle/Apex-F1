memory MGU-H: The Silent Workhorse

Sitting nestled in the 'V' of the engine block, the Motor Generator Unit - Heat (MGU-H) is arguably the most complex component. It harvests energy from the exhaust gases, spinning at upwards of 100,000 RPM. This energy is then either stored in the Energy Store (battery) or deployed directly to the MGU-K to aid acceleration.
